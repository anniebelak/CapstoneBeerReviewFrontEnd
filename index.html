WHaTs HOPs ining

Welcome To WHaTs HOPs ining, an app that allows you to review beers at your local breweries, so you can keep track of the tasty beverages you have had in your travels and remember which beers you liked best!!.

Select Local Breweries to begin reviewing the tasty beverages available at your local breweries! If you want to see your reviews or change your reviews simply select My Reviews. ENJOY!!
